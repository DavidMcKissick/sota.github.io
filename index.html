    • What's SOTA?

    SOTA is a free to play tabletop RPG system made for players looking for something a little different with a more free form class system and a heavy focus on DM freedoms.

    What does it cost?

    The core rulebook is completely free to everyone. This book is the master book for the game and will remain free throughout at least the first edition plus first edition updates. Future releases may cost a nominal fee of a couple dollars in digital format but the first edition will be around for quite a while.

    As well as this, I plan to release expansion pamphlets for new skill trees, mechanics, and so on for free during the 1.x edition cycle to provide as much free content as possible.

    Updates? On a tabletop RPG?

    Correct. A lot of games only release in whole editions which, while great for old physical formats, limits balance tweaks, fixes of various sorts, etc. So each edition will come with free updates for the edition(s) you own.

    What about the future?

    In the future I'm considering charging small amounts just to fuel further content and help with bills. This will probably start around second edition (2.x) and cost maybe 5-10 dollars at most. This wont be for a long while however as I plan to work on 1.x for a hot minute to get things nice and cleanly balanced and fun.

    Will I lose access to 1.x once 2.x comes out?

    Nope. 1.x is free for the lifetime of this project. You'll be able to play it forever and most future expansions will be compatible with 1.x. Frankly the only real reason to buy 2.x when it comes is to support my work and get a prettier presentation and some new features and tweaks.

    When does it come out?

    When it does. Alpha 1 will come out in the next few months depending on how much time I get to work on it. This edition will be a broken mess but it will at least be playable.
